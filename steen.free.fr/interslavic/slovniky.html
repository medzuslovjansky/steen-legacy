Online Slavic dictionaries

Slovjanske slovniky online

This page displays a number of Slavic online dictionaries on one page in a frame. It is intended as a tool for quickly finding the right word when a word is missing in the Interslavic dictionary. You have three possibilities:

  • If you insert an English word, it will look for this word in a number of English-Slavic dictionaries.
  • If you insert a Russian word (in Cyrillic), it will look for this word in a number of Russian-English and Russian-Slavic dictionaries.
  • If you leave the input field blank, a different set of dictionaries will be shown in a frame.
 

Insert an English or Russian search term here:
Vpišite anglijsko ilo russko slovo sde:

 

Tuta stranica pokazuje razne slovniky online v ramkah. Ona jest prěznačena k tomu, da byste legše našli i izbrali slovo, ktorogo nemaje v medžuslovjanskom slovniku. Imajete tri možnosti:

  • Ako vpišete anglijsko slovo, pokazane budut prěvody ovogo slova na razne slovjanske jezyky.
  • Ako vpišete russko slovo (kiriliceju), pokazane budut prěvody ovogo slova na anglijsky ili na druge slovjanske jezyky.
  • Ako ostavite prazdno polje, v ramě pokazany bude iny sostav slovnikov.
