Publications about Interslavic

Publikacije o medžuslovjanskom

Source: Večernje Novosti  

The Interslavic project was started in 2006 under the name „Slovianski”. From that time, several newspapers and internet in various Slavic countries have been writing about Slovianski and its sister projects Neoslavonic and Slovioski. However, Interslavic as a language is much older than that. Here follows an (incomplete) list of books and other publications about Interslavic, Slovianski, Neoslavonic and their predecessors:
